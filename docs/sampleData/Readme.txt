Sample data for cy-dataseries plugin
======================================

- Arrieta-Ortiz-et-al-2015_EV5.cys
	A regulatory network for B. subtilis as shown in Figure 5 of 
	Arrieta-Ortiz, Mario L., et al. "An experimentally supported model of the Bacillus subtilis global transcriptional regulatory network." Molecular systems biology 11.11 (2015): 839.
	http://msb.embopress.org/content/11/11/839#DC8
	
- B.subtilis-k_acetate (GDS5652).soft
	Expression of genes in B. subtilis under mild stress with potassium acetate as measured by	
	Ter Beek A, Wijman JG, Zakrzewska A, Orij R et al. Comparative physiological and transcriptional analysis of weak organic acid stress in Bacillus subtilis. Food Microbiol 2015 Feb;45(Pt A):71-82. PMID: 25481064
	SOFT file taken from Gene Expression Omnibus:
	https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/GDSbrowser?acc=GDS5652
	
- B.subtilis-germination (GSE6865).tsv
	Expression of genes in B. subtilis during germination outgrowth as measured by
	Keijser, B.J., Ter Beek, A., Rauwerda, H., Schuren, F., Montijn, R., van der Spek, H. and Brul, S. (2007) Analysis of temporal gene expression during Bacillus subtilis spore germination and outgrowth. J Bacteriol, 189, 3624-3634.
	Modified tab-separated file taken from Gene Expression Omnibus:
	https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE6865
